    About Protolabs – Digital Manufacturing & Rapid Prototyping

    Protolabs is a global digital manufacturing company offering rapid prototyping and production services across multiple technologies including 3D printing, CNC machining, injection molding, and sheet metal fabrication. With 25 years of additive manufacturing expertise and in-house facilities in the United States and Europe, Protolabs serves over 90% of Fortune 100 companies. The company specializes in delivering custom parts from concept to production with instant online quoting, design for manufacturability feedback, and lead times as fast as 1 day.

    What sets them apart

    Proprietary digital manufacturing platform with instant online quoting and real-time DFM feedback; 25 years of additive manufacturing expertise; network of 250+ manufacturing partners globally; multi-process manufacturing under one roof (3D printing, CNC, injection molding, sheet metal); domestic production in US and Europe with supply chain flexibility; ProDesk platform for streamlined quoting and order management with AI-enabled DFM analysis.
