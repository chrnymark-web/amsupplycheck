    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is Xometry the cheapest option for 3D printing?

    Not always. Xometry's pricing is competitive for US-based orders, but European suppliers like Protolabs, Sculpteo, and Craftcloud often offer better pricing for EU customers due to lower shipping costs and local production.

    Which Xometry alternative has the fastest turnaround?

    Protolabs consistently offers the fastest turnaround times, with CNC parts shipping in as little as 1 day and 3D printed parts in 1-3 days. Fictiv also offers express manufacturing services.

    Can I get instant quotes from Xometry alternatives?

    Yes. Protolabs, Hubs, Craftcloud, Fictiv, and Sculpteo all offer instant or near-instant online quoting. Upload your CAD file and get pricing within minutes.
